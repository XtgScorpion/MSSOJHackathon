Introduction

The theme for this Hackathon was to 'make lives easier during social distancing.'

I thought about all my friends and relatives that I haven't seen in months and how social distancing has made it hard to communicate.

Therefore, I developed a messaging web app to allow people to communicate with one another during these times.

Feel free to play around with the site, and have fun.
